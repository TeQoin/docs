## üîß Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Faucet says 'Rate limit exceeded'">
    You've reached the daily limit.
    
    **Solution:**
    - Wait 24 hours before requesting again
    - Try the Discord faucet as an alternative
    - Ask in Discord if you need more for testing
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="ETH not showing in wallet">
    If you requested ETH but don't see it:
    
    **Solution:**
    1. Wait 1-2 minutes (transactions take ~5 seconds, but interface may lag)
    2. Refresh your wallet (close and reopen)
    3. Make sure you're on **TeQoin L2** network (not Ethereum mainnet)
    4. Check the block explorer to confirm the transaction
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Wrong network - received ETH on Ethereum">
    Faucet only works for TeQoin L2.
    
    **Solution:**
    1. Switch to **TeQoin L2** network in your wallet
    2. Copy your address again (it's the same address, but make sure you're on the right network)
    3. Request from the faucet again
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="'Invalid address' error">
    The faucet requires a valid Ethereum-style address.
    
    **Solution:**
    1. Make sure your address starts with `0x`
    2. Check that you copied the full address (42 characters including `0x`)
    3. Don't include any spaces before or after the address
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Need more testnet ETH">
    If you need more than the daily limit for development:
    
    **Solution:**
    1. Join our [Discord](https://discord.gg/teqoin)
    2. Go to the **#developer-support** channel
    3. Explain your use case
    4. Our team can manually send additional testnet ETH
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
